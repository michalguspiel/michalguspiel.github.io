Trying to set my foot into IT.

In 2020 when Covid hit for the first time I had plenty of time because of my laid off, I quickly realized that I should be productive instead of making it a vacation. I decided to learn coding, and almost immediately fell in love with it, after few YouTube lessons I signed myself to programming course at Aalto University and it paced up my learning.

After finishing the course I decided to focus on mobile app development and make it my specialization, I accomplished few hobby projects, one of which is on PlayStore and has over 4 thousands downloads and over 1000 active devices.

Now I study BIT, Web and Mobile App development on Laurea UAS.

The reason and motivation

The reason why I got interested particulary in mobile development is that phone is something that I always got with me and I can trully understand the purpose of different applications as a user.

Secondly, it is easy to notice that mobile app development is a huge market. If You look around You for example in public transport, it is difficult to find someone who isn't staring in the screen of a smartphone.

Finally, mobile app development is an interesting area where individual can develop a breakthrough product.
